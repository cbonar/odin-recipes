Velveeta Broccoli Cheese Soup

The Perfect Recipe for the Perfect Soup for Chilly Winter Days!

Want to bring back memories from your childhood? How about you have the sniffles and don't feel good? Maybe it's just a snowy day. Whatever the reason this soup will make you feel warm and cozy. Make sure you try this delicious and easy one-pot soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 1 white onion (finely diced)
  • 2 bags frozen chopped broccoli
  • 4 cans chicken broth (14.5 oz each)
  • 16 oz. Velveeta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

Instructions

  1. In a large soup pot, over medium high heat, add the butter and chopped onion. Cook until softened, stirring frequently to prevent burning, for about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the frozen chopped broccoli and chicken broth (Reserve 1 cup for cornstarch slurry). Bring to a boil, you may have to increase the heat as needed, once boiling reduce heat to medium add let simmer for 20 minutes or until broccoli is softened to your liking.
  3. Add the Velveeta cheese, shredded cheese, half and half, and garlic powder. Stir together and let it cook for about 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and combined.
  4. Combine the cornstarch and reserved 1 cup chicken broth in a small bowl. stir with a fork until mixed. Add into soup and let cook for about 5-10 minutes or until the soup is thick enough to your liking.
  5. Serve immediately. Try topping it off with some additional shredded cheese.
  6. Store leftovers in an airt-tight container for 3-4 days in the fridge. This soup can also be frozen as well. Heat up frozen leftovers in a soup pot with some additional water (like 1/2 cup or so).
