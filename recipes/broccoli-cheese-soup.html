Velveeta Broccoli Cheese Soup

The Perfect Recipe for the Perfect Soup for Chilly Winter Days!

Want to bring back memories from your childhood? How about you have the sniffles and don't feel good? Maybe it's just a snowy day. Whatever the reason this soup will make you feel warm and cozy. Make sure you try this delicious and easy one-pot soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 1 white onion (finely diced)
  • 2 bags frozen chopped broccoli
  • 4 cans chicken broth (14.5 oz each)
  • 16 oz. Velveeta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
