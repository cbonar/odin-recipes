Mocha Cupcakes

An Explosion on Your Tounge for Both Coffee and Chocolate Lovers!

This delicious recipe is perfect for busy mornings or a late-night sweet tooth! You get the boost of coffee and knock your chocolate craving all in one bite. The moist and fluffy cake mixed with the smooth espresso buttercream frosting is something to die for! Watch out! You have to be careful with these or you might just eat the whole batch, they're so yummy!

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1/2 cup brewed coffee, at room tempurature
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons espresso powder
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 egg, at room temperature

For the Espresso Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons espresso powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Line a standard sized muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. For the Cupcakes: In a measuring cup, whisk the espresso powder into the brewed coffee until completely dissolved. Add the milk and the vanilla extract; set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. With an electric mixer on medium speed, beat the butter and both sugars until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add the egg and beat until combined. Reduce the mixer speed to low and add the flour mixture in three additions, alternating with two additions of coffee mixture, mixing each until just combined. Using a rubber spatula, scrape the sides and make sure everything is well incorporated.
  5. Fill each muffin cup about two-thirds full with batter. Bake for 17-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely before frosting.
  6. For the Frosting: In a small bowl, whisk the espresso powder into the vanilla until dissolved; set aside.
  7. Using the whisk attatchment of a stand mixer, whip the butter on medium-high speed for about 5 minutes, stopping once to scrape the sides of the bowl. Reduce the powdered sugar a little at a time, waiting until it is mostly incorporated before adding more. Once all of the powdered sugar has been added, scrape the sides of the bowl and increase the speed to medium-high speed and whip until fluffy, about 1 to 2 minutes. Add the espresso and vanilla mixture and continue to mix at medium-high speeds until it is completely incorporated, scraping the sides as necessary. Frost as desired and enjoy!
