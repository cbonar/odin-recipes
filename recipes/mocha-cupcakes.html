Mocha Cupcakes

An Explosion on Your Tounge for Both Coffee and Chocolate Lovers!

This delicious recipe is perfect for busy mornings or a late-night sweet tooth! You get the boost of coffee and knock your chocolate craving all in one bite. The moist and fluffy cake mixed with the smooth espresso buttercream frosting is something to die for! Watch out! You have to be careful with these or you might just eat the whole batch, they're so yummy!

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1/2 cup brewed coffee, at room tempurature
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons espresso powder
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 egg, at room temperature
